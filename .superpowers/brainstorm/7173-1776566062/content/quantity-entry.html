A — Tap to Add, Badge Count

Each tap adds +1. A red badge on the tile shows the count. Long-press the tile to remove. Fast for single or few items — no extra screen.

B — Tap Opens Quantity Modal

Tapping a tile opens a popup with +/− buttons and shows the subtotal before adding. Slightly slower but explicit — good when ordering multiple of one item.

C — Tap Adds to Live Cart List

Tapping adds to a cart list below the tiles with inline +/− controls. Order is always visible. Good for multi-item orders.
