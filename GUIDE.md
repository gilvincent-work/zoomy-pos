# ZoomyPOS — How to Use the App

---

## The Main Screen

When you open the app, you'll see the **POS screen** — this is where you take orders.

- The list shows all your active products
- Use **+** and **−** to add or remove items from the current order
- The total updates automatically at the bottom
- Tap **Charge** when you're ready to collect payment

---

## Taking Payment

After tapping **Charge**, choose how the customer is paying:

### Cash
1. Select **Cash**
2. Tap the bills the customer handed you (or tap **Exact** if they gave the exact amount)
3. The app calculates the change for you
4. Optionally enter the customer's furbaby or IG handle
5. Tap **Confirm Sale**

### GCash / Bank Transfer
1. Select **GCash** or **Bank**
2. Show the QR code to the customer (tap **Full Screen** to make it bigger)
3. Tap **Customer Paid ✓** once they've sent the money
4. Optionally enter the reference number and take a photo of the receipt
5. Optionally enter the customer's furbaby or IG handle
6. Tap **Confirm Sale** — or **Skip** if you don't have proof yet

---

## Bundles

Use bundles when a customer is buying a set of items for one fixed price.

### One-time bundle
1. Tap **Bundle** at the bottom of the screen
2. Pick the items and quantities in the bundle
3. Enter the bundle price
4. Tap **Add to Cart** — then proceed to Charge as usual

### Saving a bundle preset
If you sell the same bundle often, save it so you don't have to rebuild it every time:
1. Tap **Bundle**, pick the items and set the price
2. Tap **Save** instead of Add to Cart
3. Give it a name and tap **OK**
4. It will now appear as a quick-tap button below the product list

### Using a saved bundle preset
- Tap the preset chip to add it to the order
- Tap it again to add another of the same bundle
- Tap **−** on the chip to remove one

---

## Managing Products

Tap the **📦 box icon** in the top-right corner to manage your products.

- **Add a product** — tap the **+** button, fill in the name, price, and emoji
- **Edit a product** — tap on any product to update it
- **Deactivate a product** — toggle it off so it doesn't show on the POS screen (your history is kept)
- **Variants / Flavors** — if a product comes in different flavors or sizes, you can add variants under it. The customer will be asked to pick one when ordering.

---

## Transaction History

Tap the **🧾 receipt icon** in the top-right corner to see all sales.

- Tap any transaction to see its full details
- Use the **filter buttons** at the top to view Today, This Week, or All Time
- Tap **Export** to download a ZIP file with a CSV of all transactions and any attached receipt photos
- To **void a transaction**, open it and tap Void (requires admin PIN)

---

## Admin Settings

Tap the **⚙️ gear icon** in the top-right corner.

You'll be asked for your PIN first (default is **0000**).

Inside settings you can:
- **Change your PIN**
- **Upload your GCash QR code** — this is the QR that gets shown to customers during GCash payments

---

## Tips

- **Data is saved on your device** — closing the app won't lose anything
- **Works without internet** — once the app is installed and opened once on WiFi, it runs fully offline
- **Multiple bundles + individual items** — you can mix bundle presets and regular products in the same order
- **Long-press a bundle preset** to delete it from the list
